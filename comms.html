GMRS vs ham: GMRS is the simplest path to family/neighborhood comms — $35 FCC license, no exam, covers everyone in your household. Midland T71 and similar radios work with your CHIRP channels. Ham radio has more power, more bands, and better emergency infrastructure, but GMRS is a solid fallback.

🔌 Antenna Guide

Balun One Nine + WireHF 0.5–30 MHz
Red wire (7.5m) → ANT post · Black wire (2.5m) → GND post. Also usable for VHF low-band (47–160 MHz) public safety monitoring.
ANT500 TelescopicVHF/UHF
Extend to ~44 cm for NOAA (162 MHz) and 2m repeaters. Best antenna for public safety + ham monitoring.
V4 Dipole KitSatellites / ADS-B
54 cm per element for NOAA satellites (137 MHz) · Short elements for ADS-B (1090 MHz).
